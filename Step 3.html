Cough or sneeze into your elbow

Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue when you cough or sneeze. Throw used tissues in the trash. If you don't have a tissue, cough or sneeze into your elbow, not your hands.

Remember to immediately wash your hands after blowing your nose, coughing or sneezing.

Washing your hands is one of the most effective ways to prevent yourself and your loved ones from getting sick, especially at key times when you are likely to get and spread germs. Wash your hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds If soap and water are not readily available, use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol to clean hands
